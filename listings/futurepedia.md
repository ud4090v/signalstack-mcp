**Tool Name:** SignalStack

**Tagline:** Daily crypto market intelligence — 0-100 score from real data, built for developers and AI agents.

**Description:**
SignalStack is a daily crypto market intelligence platform that scores current market conditions on a 0-100 scale. Every day, it pulls live data across 7 market categories — price action, on-chain activity, derivatives, ETF flows, macro, dominance, and sentiment — and distills it into a single Candle Review score with label, category breakdowns, and narrative analysis.

**Key features:**
- **Composite Candle Review score:** 0-100, updated daily at 06:00 UTC
- **7-category breakdown:** Understand which signals are bullish or bearish
- **Daily narrative:** "What happened today" and "what to watch next"
- **AI agent ready:** MCP server via SSE transport — one tool call for today's market regime
- **REST API + OpenAPI:** Standard REST endpoints with published OpenAPI 3.0 spec
- **Freemium:** Score + label free, full breakdown for pro subscribers

**Use cases:**
- Portfolio dashboards displaying market regime at a glance
- Trading systems gated on market conditions
- AI agents needing structured market context in their reasoning
- Research and backtesting with historical score data

**Pricing:**
- Free tier: composite score + label (no API key required)
- Pro tier: full category breakdown, outlook, narrative ($19/mo)
